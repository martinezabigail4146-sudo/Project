From Bean to Brew — The History of Coffee

A concise timeline of coffee's journey through time.

  1. 9th century — Origins in Ethiopia (legend of Kaldi the goat herder).
  2. 15th–16th century — Coffee houses across the Middle East and Europe.
  3. 17th–18th century — Coffee plantations spread globally.
  4. 20th century — Specialty coffee movements arise.
